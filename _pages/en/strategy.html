--- title: Monitoring of the German Sustainability Strategy by the Federal Statistical Office permalink: /en/about/dns/strategy/ language: en layout: page --- The target values to be achieved as part of the German Sustainable Development Strategy, the indicators, their data bases and calculations as well as the graphical representations have been decided by the Federal Government. The Federal Statistical Office was also involved in an advisory capacity. The structure of the German Sustainable Development Strategy and the selection of indicators are based on the United Nations 2030 Agenda and its 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). Accordingly, at least one indicator for each of the 17 goals was included in the German Sustainable goals was included in the German Sustainable Development Strategy. Since 2006, the Federal Statistical Office has been tasked with supporting the measurement of national indicators with its own data from official statistics and coordinating the integration of data from other authorities. For this reason, in addition to the Sustainable Development Strategy indicators now published on this online platform, the Federal Statistical Office publishes an indicator report for the German Sustainable Development Strategy every two years (most recently in 2022).
